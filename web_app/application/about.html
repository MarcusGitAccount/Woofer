What We Do

We are committed to create a matchmaking heaven for dogs with pedigrees, all around the world. It's as easy as it can get! With a click of a button, we find the best matches for your dog, at just a throw of a stick from your home!

Why We Do It

Many people love their dogs and spend a lot of time with them. We want to create opportunities for your K-9 friend, using the power of Internet, thus making matchmaking easy-peasy-lemon-squeezy!

How It Works

It just does! We find your dog's race, using an advanced image recognition. Then, we recommend you with matches of the same pedigree, located near you. This is done using a database which grows over time. All you need to do is follow a few, very simple steps!

Find the best match for your dog, close to your location, all around the world.

So, where do I start?

Just click the Get Started button in the navigation bar. If you don't have an account, you can create one in a few minutes. If you already have an account, then you can login. Then, you can start searching!

The data you entered, will be collected and used to further expand our network of dogs, including their pedigrees. Your dog's genetic inheritance is valuable. Make sure it goes to the right place!
